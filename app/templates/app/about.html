What is Coupon Bazaar?

Coupon Bazaar is India's first dedicated marketplace for buying and selling unused coupons and discount codes. We believe every coupon deserves to be used. Whether it is a brand loyalty reward you cannot redeem, a gift card you will not use, or a promotional code that is about to expire, Coupon Bazaar gives it a second life.

For sellers: Instead of letting your coupons expire worthless, list them on our platform and earn money. Set your own price and reach thousands of eager buyers who are looking for exactly that deal.

For buyers: Why pay full price when someone else has a discount they are not going to use? Browse hundreds of verified coupons across fashion, food, electronics, travel, and more -- all at prices well below face value.
